Instructions

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Enter Player Name.
2. Press Start Match button, hear a cat meow twice, and a toast will appear, game is ready.
3. 16 Cards are shown with faces down, player must match them up.
4. Player picks 2 cards, they flip and if they are a match cards will stop rotating with a happy cat meow.
5. If the cards do not match they will flip back face down.
6. Game is complete when all cards are paired and there is a toast will appear.
